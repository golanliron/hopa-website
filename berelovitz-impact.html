Hadassah Neurim Youth Village · Rehabilitative Kennel

The patrol has been operating since 2008 and to date has responded to the distress of hundreds of thousands of youth. The grant was extremely significant for expanding the patrol's activities, enabling us to strengthen proactive response precisely at a time when the need is growing. From the field we clearly see that young people are dealing with ongoing mental fatigue. In such reality, the patrol has a critical role: not to wait for people in distress to reach out themselves, but to identify signs of distress in time and provide assistance proactively. We wish to thank the Berelowitz Family Foundation for the generous grant.
